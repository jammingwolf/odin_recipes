The Best Damn Lasagna

Description

The well known NYC posh restaurant Don Angie's may be the uncontested winner of best tasting lasagna. As pictured above, they are famous for the unconventional shape inspired by cinnamon buns. If you would like to learn their secrets, keep scrolling!

Ingredients

  • Four
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Yellow Onion
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Add eggs and flour
  2. Chill dough
  3. Roll dough thin
  4. Boil dough for 2 seconds
  5. Chill overnight
  6. Make tomato sauce
  7. Place tomato sauce
  8. Place thin layer of riccotta and mozzarella
  9. Roll up the pieces
  10. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees
  11. Enjoy!
