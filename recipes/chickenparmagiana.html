Chicken Parmagiana Your Grandmother Would Approve of

Description

Crunchy, rich, salty, this chicken parmagiana recipe is going to have you wanting seconds.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Parmagiana
  • Mozzarella
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Clean and bread the chicken cutlets
  2. Fry the chicken cutlets in olive oil
  3. Top the fried cutlets with sauce, mozzarella, and parmagiana
  4. Bake at 300 for 30 minutes
