Lemon Scallop Crudo

Description

Bright and zesty, this dish will satisfy any craving for scallops.

Ingredients

  • Scallops
  • Lemon
  • soy sauce
  • ginger
  • Sherry
  • mint
  • chives

Steps

  1. Clean scallops
  2. Combine lemon, soy sauce, ginger, mint
  3. Top scallops with sauce
  4. Plate with chives as a garnish
